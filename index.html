關於我們 | About Us

我們的使命是推動人工智慧安全和倫理在台灣的發展，確保人工智慧技術在各領域的應用符合道德標準，並減少風險。我們的願景是成為台灣人工智慧安全領域的領導者，促進相關研究、教育和公共政策的進步。

Our mission is to promote AI safety and ethics development in Taiwan, ensuring that AI applications across various fields meet ethical standards while minimizing risks. Our vision is to become a leader in Taiwan's AI safety field, advancing related research, education, and public policy.

AI Safety TW 成立於 2023 年，由一群熱衷於人工智慧安全和倫理的台大學生組成。我們致力於提高社會對人工智慧風險的認識，並推動相關的工作坊、讀書會的進行。

Founded in 2023, AI Safety TW consists of NTU students passionate about AI safety and ethics. We are committed to raising awareness about AI risks in society and organizing workshops and reading groups.
