組織使命｜Our Mission

我們的使命是推動人工智慧安全和倫理在台灣的發展，確保人工智慧技術在各領域的應用符合道德標準，並減少風險。我們的願景是成為台灣人工智慧安全領域的領導者，促進相關研究、教育和公共政策的進步。作為一個新興且快速發展的領域，我們透過提供高品質的基礎建設，包括工作坊、競賽活動、社群聚會等，積極支持台灣的機器學習社群投入 AI Safety 研究。

Our mission is to promote AI safety and ethics development in Taiwan, ensuring that AI applications across various fields meet ethical standards while minimizing risks. Our vision is to become a leader in Taiwan's AI safety field, advancing related research, education, and public policy. As an emerging and rapidly evolving field, we actively support Taiwan's machine learning community in pursuing AI safety research by providing high-quality infrastructure, including workshops, competitions, and social events.

AI Safety Taiwan 成立於 2023 年，由一群熱衷於人工智慧安全和倫理的台大學生組成。我們致力於提高社會對人工智慧風險的認識，並推動相關的工作坊、讀書會的進行。透過建立完善的研究資源與基礎建設，我們致力於降低入門門檻，建立跨領域交流平台，並推動這個領域的發展。我們相信，只有凝聚更多優秀人才投入這個領域，才能真正應對 AI 發展帶來的挑戰。

Founded in 2023, AI Safety Taiwan consists of NTU students passionate about AI safety and ethics. We are committed to raising awareness about AI risks in society and organizing workshops and reading groups. By providing comprehensive research resources and infrastructure, we strive to lower barriers to entry and establish platforms for interdisciplinary exchange. We believe that only by bringing together talented individuals from diverse backgrounds can we effectively address the challenges posed by AI development.
