AI Safety Taiwan: ARENA 3.0 Workshop

2025 年 8 月 2-3 日及 8 月 16-17 日，台大德田館｜August 2-3 & 16-17, 2025, NTU Detan Hall

深入學習 AI 安全性與對齊技術的實作工作坊。從 Transformer 架構基礎到機制解釋性分析，再到強化學習與大語言模型評估。兩天密集課程包含理論講解與實作練習，適合有 Python 基礎並對 AI 安全領域有興趣的學員參加。

An intensive hands-on workshop on AI Safety and Alignment techniques. Covering fundamentals from Transformer architecture to Mechanistic Interpretability, Reinforcement Learning, and LLM Evaluation. Two-day intensive program with theory and practical exercises, suitable for participants with Python background interested in AI Safety.

EAGxSingapore 2024

2024 年 12 月 14-15 日，新加坡｜December 14-15, 2024, Singapore

EAGxSingapore 是一場針對東南亞地區有效利他主義社群成員和相關組織的會議，預計將有 200-350 名與會者參加。活動將包括演講、工作坊、小組討論和社交活動，旨在平衡入門級和進階的有效利他主義內容。

EAGxSingapore is a conference aimed at Effective Altruism community members and aligned organizations in Southeast Asia, expecting 200-350 attendees. The event will feature talks, workshops, small group discussions, and networking opportunities, balancing accessible and advanced EA content.
