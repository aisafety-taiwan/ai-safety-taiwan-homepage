EAGxSingapore 2024

2024 年 12 月 14-15 日，新加坡｜December 14-15, 2024, Singapore

EAGxSingapore 是一場針對東南亞地區有效利他主義社群成員和相關組織的會議，預計將有 200-350 名與會者參加。活動將包括演講、工作坊、小組討論和社交活動，旨在平衡入門級和進階的有效利他主義內容。

EAGxSingapore is a conference aimed at Effective Altruism community members and aligned organizations in Southeast Asia, expecting 200-350 attendees. The event will feature talks, workshops, small group discussions, and networking opportunities, balancing accessible and advanced EA content.
